I am a recent graduate of Eastern Washington University. I earned a BA in Computer Science and a minor in Web Development. Since graduating, I have been developing my web design skills as well as acquiring new ones. The most frustrating yet interesting aspect about web development is that it is constantly changing, so you are always in a state of learning.

I am a gamer. I love playing video games. Since I did not get the chance to take any courses on Game Development when I was at Eastern, I decided to take an online Game Development course from Coursera. I really enjoyed everything that class had to offer and have been motivated to learn more on the subject.

Other than playing video games, I enjoy reading, watching television, playing with my dog. I also happen to be a professional at assembling IKEA furniture.
